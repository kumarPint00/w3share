## üîë Connecting Your Wallet

### Step-by-Step Connection
1. **Click "Connect Wallet"** on the DogeGift homepage

> üí° **Security Note**: Never share your private keys or seed phrases with anyone, including DogeGift.

## üéÅ Creating Your First Gift

### The Gift Creation Process

#### Step 1: Start Creating
1. Click **"Create Gift"** from the dashboard
2. Choose between **"Quick Gift"** or **"Custom Gift"**

#### Step 2: Select Assets
1. **View your assets** - See all your ERC20 tokens and NFTs
2. **Choose what to gift** - Click on tokens or NFTs to select them
3. **Set amounts** - Enter how much of each token to send

#### Step 3: Add Personalization
1. **Write a message** - Add a personal note (optional)
2. **Set expiry date** - Choose when the gift expires
3. **Create secret code** - This protects your gift

#### Step 4: Review and Lock
1. **Review all details** - Check assets, amounts, and settings
2. **Confirm transaction** - Approve in MetaMask
3. **Wait for confirmation** - Your gift is now locked on blockchain!

#### Step 5: Share Your Gift
1. **Copy the share link** - This contains your gift ID
2. **Share securely** - Send the link and secret code separately
3. **Track status** - Monitor if your gift gets claimed

## üéØ Claiming a Gift

### The Claiming Process

#### Step 1: Access the Gift
1. **Click the gift link** you received
2. **Review gift details** - See what's inside
3. **Connect your wallet** if not already connected

#### Step 2: Enter Secret Code
1. **Type the secret code** provided by the sender
2. **Click "Claim Gift"**
3. **Wait for processing** - This happens instantly!

#### Step 3: Receive Your Assets
1. **Assets arrive automatically** in your wallet
2. **Check your balance** - Tokens/NFTs should appear
3. **Transaction complete** - You're done!

### What Happens During Claim
- **Code verification** - Smart contract checks the code
- **Asset transfer** - Tokens move from escrow to your wallet
- **Status update** - Gift marked as claimed
- **No gas fees** - We cover the transaction costs

## ‚ùì Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### "Transaction Failed"
- **Check gas**: Ensure you have enough ETH for gas
- **Network**: Make sure you're on Sepolia testnet
- **Wallet**: Ensure MetaMask is unlocked and connected

#### "Gift Not Found"
- **Check link**: Ensure the URL is complete and correct
- **Network**: Verify you're on the right blockchain
- **Expiry**: Gifts expire and become unavailable

#### "Invalid Code"
- **Check spelling**: Codes are case-sensitive
- **Complete code**: Ensure you have the full code
- **Single use**: Codes can only be used once

#### "Assets Not Received"
- **Wait time**: Blockchain transactions take ~15 seconds
- **Check wallet**: Ensure correct wallet is connected
- **Network**: Verify you're on the right network
